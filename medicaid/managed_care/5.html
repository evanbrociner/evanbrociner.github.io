Section 4: The Role of Data, Technology, and Collaboration

  • Data-Driven Decision Making: The use of data analytics to track patient outcomes and identify areas for improvement is essential for refining care management programs. (Managing Cost of Care: Lessons from Successful Organizations)
  • Technology for Improved Care: Implementing technologies like electronic health records and telemedicine can enhance communication, coordination, and patient engagement.
  • Collaboration for Success: Collaboration among stakeholders, including MCOs, providers, community-based organizations, and patients, is crucial for developing and implementing effective care management programs. (Reframing Value-Based Care Management Beyond Cost Reduction and Toward Patient Centerednesse)
