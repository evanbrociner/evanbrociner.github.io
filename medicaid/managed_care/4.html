Section 3: Integrated Care Models for Holistic Support

Integrated care models, such as CareMore, offer a promising approach to improving care for Medicaid beneficiaries. These models:

  • Address Medical and Social Needs: By addressing both medical and social needs, integrated care models like CareMore can improve outcomes and reduce costs for various populations. (Rethinking How Medicaid Patients Receive Care)
  • Break Down Silos: They break down traditional silos between inpatient and outpatient care, ensuring continuity of care and addressing patients' needs holistically.
  • Focus on Community and Patient Engagement: By involving patients in their own care and connecting them with community resources, these models can improve patient satisfaction and reduce hospital readmissions.
